今年美国数学奥林匹克第二天的几何题（P2）的解答，顺带测试新上线的DeepSeek-V4-Pro模型。

1. 题目

Let ABCABCABC be a triangle. Points DDD, EEE, and FFF lie on sides BCBCBC, CACACA, and ABABAB, respectively, such that

∠AFE=∠BDF=∠CED. \angle AFE=\angle BDF=\angle CED. ∠AFE=∠BDF=∠CED.

Let OAO_AOA​, OBO_BOB​, and OCO_COC​ be the circumcenters of triangles AFEAFEAFE, BDFBDFBDF, and CEDCEDCED, respectively. Let MMM, NNN, and OOO be the circumcenters of triangles ABCABCABC, DEFDEFDEF, and OAOBOCO_AO_BO_COA​OB​OC​, respectively. Prove that OM=ONOM=ONOM=ON.

2. 翻译

在 △ABC\triangle ABC△ABC 中，点 DDD、EEE、FFF 分别在边 BCBCBC、CACACA、ABABAB 上，且满足 ∠AFE=∠BDF=∠CED\angle AFE = \angle BDF = \angle CED∠AFE=∠BDF=∠CED．设 OAO_AOA​、OBO_BOB​、OCO_COC​ 分别为 △AEF\triangle AEF△AEF、△BFD\triangle BFD△BFD、△CDE\triangle CDE△CDE 的外心，MMM、NNN、OOO 分别为 △ABC\triangle ABC△ABC、△DEF\triangle DEF△DEF、△OAOBOC\triangle O_AO_BO_C△OA​OB​OC​ 的外心，求证：OM=ONOM=ONOM=ON．
